## SIRS Compliance: What's at Stake?

### Understanding SIRS Regulations

The Florida SIRS regulations mandate comprehensive structural inspections and necessary repairs for condominium associations. These regulations were enacted to prevent catastrophic structural failures and ensure the safety and longevity of residential buildings. By 2026, all affected associations must comply with these standards, which often reveal significant repair needs.
